Synopsis

The murder case of Madame D, one of the world's richest person!
The culprit is Gustave, the legendary hotel manager and her lover?!

One day in 1917, when World War II was in full swing, Madame D, the world's richest woman, was mysteriously murdered not long after visiting the Grand Budapest Hotel. In her will, Madame D leaves the famous painting "Boy with an Apple", which has been passed down in the family for generations, to her lover Gustave, the legendary hotel manager. His son Dmitri, who was seeking Madame D’s inheritance, suddenly points out Gustave as a prime suspect and hires the ruthless killer, Jopling, to take over the Grand Budapest Hotel along with Madame D’s belongings. To clear his name, Gustave begins an extraordinary adventure with a faithful hotel lobby boy, Zero. Will Gustave and Zero be able to survive from Dmitri, prove their innocence, and safely inherit the painting?
